# Real Estate Website Design Palette

## Color Palette Options

### Option 1: Sophisticated Neutrals (Recommended)
- **Primary**: #2C3E50 (Deep Blue-Gray) - Professional, trustworthy
- **Secondary**: #34495E (Slate Gray) - Sophisticated accent
- **Accent**: #E67E22 (Warm Orange) - Premium, inviting
- **Background**: #FAFAFA (Warm White) - Clean, spacious
- **Text**: #2C3E50 (High contrast, readable)
- **Success**: #27AE60 (Property available)
- **Warning**: #F39C12 (Contact actions)

**Rationale**: This palette conveys luxury and professionalism while maintaining warmth. The deep blue-gray suggests stability and trust, essential for real estate. The warm orange accent adds premium feel without being overwhelming.

### Option 2: Modern Earth Tones
- **Primary**: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown) - Grounded, natural
- **Secondary**: #A0522D (Sienna) - Warm, approachable
- **Accent**: #CD853F (Peru) - Elegant highlight
- **Background**: #FFF8DC (Cornsilk) - Soft, welcoming
- **Text**: #2F1B14 (Dark brown for contrast)
- **Success**: #6B8E23 (Olive Drab)
- **Warning**: #DAA520 (Goldenrod)

**Rationale**: Earth tones connect with property and land themes, creating a natural, trustworthy feeling. Perfect for real estate focusing on residential properties and land sales.

### Option 3: Premium Monochrome
- **Primary**: #1A1A1A (Rich Black) - Ultra-modern
- **Secondary**: #4A4A4A (Dark Gray) - Professional depth
- **Accent**: #C0A062 (Gold) - Luxury accent
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (Pure White) - Clean minimalism
- **Text**: #1A1A1A (Strong contrast)
- **Success**: #2E7D32 (Dark Green)
- **Warning**: #F57C00 (Orange)

**Rationale**: High-contrast monochrome with gold accents creates an ultra-premium feel. Ideal for luxury properties and high-end real estate markets.

## Typography
- **Display Font**: Playfair Display (Elegant serif for headings)
- **Body Font**: Inter (Clean sans-serif for readability)
- **Farsi Font**: Vazirmatn (Professional Persian font)

## Visual Language
- Clean, spacious layouts with generous white space
- Professional photography emphasis
- Subtle shadows and depth for premium feel
- Consistent grid system throughout
- Elegant hover states and transitions

## Implementation Notes
- All colors maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios
- RTL layout will mirror all horizontal spacing
- Farsi typography will use larger line heights
- Color coding for property status (Available/Sold/Pending)
